TIMESTAMP MICROSERVICE

This is a Timestamp Microservice. It can be called with a natural date(2015-12-25 or Dec 25, 2015) or unix date timestamp(1451001600000) as a parameter and returns a JSON with both the unix time stamp and natural language form of the given date.

To see the current date and time add: /date

To see any other date and time, follow the examples below.

Example:

  • /date/2015-12-25
  • /date/Dec 25, 2015
  • /date/1451001600000

Result:

{"unix":1451001600000, "utc":"Fri, 25 Dec 2015 00:00:00 GMT"}
